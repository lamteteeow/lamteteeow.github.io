# The Macro Pulse: Recession Risk Dashboard

**The Macro Pulse** is a simple dashboard designed to visualize three critical macroeconomic indicators to assess the risk and proximity of an economic recession. 

🌍 **Live Dashboard:** [https://lamteteeow.github.io](https://lamteteeow.github.io)

## Indicators Tracked

1. **Yield Curve Spread (10Y - 2Y)**
   Measures the difference between 10-year and 2-year Treasury yields. A negative spread (inversion) is a classic leading indicator of a recession.

2. **Sahm Rule Recession Indicator**
   Signals the start of a recession when the three-month moving average of the national unemployment rate rises by 0.50 percentage points or more relative to its low during the previous 12 months.

3. **Buffett Indicator (Market Cap / GDP)**
   Compares total stock market valuation to GDP. An exceptionally high ratio suggests the market may be overvalued and vulnerable to a downturn.

---
*Data is automatically fetched from the FRED (Federal Reserve Economic Data) API.*
